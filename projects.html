Farm.io - Farm Security Surveillance

Farm.io is an innovative motion detector device designed to enhance Farm security and prevent intrusions. It detects motion and alerts farmers of potential threats in real-time, ensuring prompt action can be taken to protect valuable assets. Advanced features include
1. Motion detection technology to detect intrusion with instant email alerts sent to the farmer upon intrusion.
2. Alarm can only be turned off by authorised staff members using a fingerprint sensor. Email alerts include details of the staff member who turned off the alarm.

Stegno - Voice Encryption Tool

Stegno uses concepts learned from Signals and Systems to encrypt audio signals.
The program was developed with Python alongside some of its libraries such as Tkinter, Numpy and Matplotlib
The encryption methods used are as explained :
1. Reversal: Here, the audio signal is reversed to produce unintelligible sound, hence encrypting it.
2. Noise addition: Random noise is generated and added to the audio signal. This leads to the original signal not being heard clearly.

Heart Beat Monitor

A low cost heart beat monitor that uses the concepts of filters and amplifiers as learned in the Instrumentation for Engineering course. The movement of the blood through the blood vessels is filtered and then amplified to check the heart beat.
The system uses :

1. Filters to remove noise from the blood movement through the blood vessels.
2. Amplifiers to amplify the heartbeat and make it easy to be analysed.

Fingerprint Attendance System

By the use of a biometeric fingerprint sensor, the system records matches the fingerprint used against the ones in the database. If a match is detected, the name of the user is displayed along with the time of arrival and simultaneously saved in a csv file which can be accessed by the faculty. The lock is subsequently opened for the student.

This system, by the use of a biometric prevents students signing in for others and reduces the workload on faculty when recording attendance for a large class.
