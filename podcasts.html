The 7MA Show

Welcome to my podcast, where curiousity meets conversation. I explore the intersection of technology, politics, and student life, tackling a wide range of topics that shape our world. From in-depth discussions on current events, social issues and technological advancements to personal stories of student experiences, triumphs and struggles, I invite listeners to join me on a journey of discovery and connection.

With a curious and open-minded approach, I explore new ideas, challenge perspectives and spark meaningful discussions. Whether you are a student, a professional or simply someone who loves to learn, my podcast is a space for thoughtful conversation, inspiration, and community. Tune in for thought-provoking episodes that educate, entertain and empower.
